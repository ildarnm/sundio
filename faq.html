Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

Q: How do I add an expense?

Tap the "+" button on the main screen, enter the amount, select a category, and tap Save.

Q: How do I create a budget?

Go to Budgets tab, tap "Add Budget", set your limit and time period (weekly/monthly), then save.

Q: How do I organize expenses into groups?

Create groups (like "Food", "Transport") in the Groups section, then assign expenses to groups when adding them.

Managing Your Data

Q: How do I export my data?

Go to Settings > Export Data. You can download your expenses as a file for your records.

Q: How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. This will permanently remove all your data. This action cannot be undone.

Q: Is my data synced across devices?

Yes, when you sign in with your account, your data syncs automatically across all your devices.

Troubleshooting

Q: The app won't sync my data

Check your internet connection and try again. If the problem persists, try signing out and back in.

Q: I forgot my password

On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password" and enter your email. You'll receive a link to reset your password.

Q: My expenses are not showing

Make sure you're viewing the correct date range. Pull down to refresh the list.
